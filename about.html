About the Show

Brain Soup is a podcast about neurodivergent brains navigating systems that weren't built for them — and the weird, brilliant workarounds that emerge when you stop trying to fit.

Hosted by Kate Chapman and That Damn Tee, two geo/tech veterans who've spent careers building teams, hacking bureaucracies, and collecting too many side quests.
